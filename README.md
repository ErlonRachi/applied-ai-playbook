# Applied AI Playbook

Opinions and patterns from 20+ years of bringing data and AI into
enterprises — now refactored for the agentic era.

## What this is

Short, opinionated notes on how to actually deliver AI value in
established organizations. Not a framework. Not a consultancy pitch.
A living collection of the things I keep telling clients, written
down so I stop repeating myself.

## Current entries

- `on-adoption/` — why most AI pilots don't scale, and what to do
  about it
- `on-teams/` — staffing AI functions at different organizational
  stages
- `on-governance/` — the governance posture that doesn't strangle
  experimentation
- `on-agentic/` — what's actually different about agentic AI, and
  what isn't
